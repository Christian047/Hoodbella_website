    PLACES TO VISIT

    Lagos Island

    The historic heart of Nigeria's economic capital, Lagos Island presents a fascinating blend of commerce and culture. Experience premium dining at the Federal Palace Hotel or embrace contemporary Nigerian art at Terra Kulture.

    Lekki Peninsula

     A masterpiece of modern Lagos, Lekki combines luxury with leisure. The award-winning Nike Art Gallery houses Nigeria's finest contemporary artworks, while the Lekki Conservation Centre offers Africa's longest canopy walkway

    Pretoria,Cape Town

     A sophisticated capital draped in purple blooms, Pretoria balances diplomatic grandeur with cultural authenticity. The iconic Union Buildings, set against manicured gardens, stand as a testament to South Africa's democracy.
